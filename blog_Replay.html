Replay Experience in TikTok 2020.2.19
 Laughter BGM has become a typical sound of TikTok, you can hear that sound in many public spots, on the bus, on the metro, in restaurants, on streets and so on. Myself is not a user of TikTok, so when I heard the BGM of TikTok playing over and over again, I used to think the person is watching that video over and over again.
  Recently, my mom watching videos on TikTok and a variety of TikTok symbolic BGM played over and over again. I am a little bit confused and I observe her when she using TikTok. I found out it is not her behavior, it is because TikTok plays videos over and over again automatically, even when she is viewing the comments of the videos. And she apparently doesn't notice that BGM could disrupt others when she is absorbed in the funny comments.

  Since the users do not pay attention to the video while reading comments, why not mute video to not bother others. I assume the reasons why TikTok loops videos as follow:
  1. TikTok's videos mostly have a span under a minute, looping video could help the user know the content of it if videos contain much information.
  2. Not only videos, but the community is an important part of TikTok. My mom often takes much more time to read comments than watch short videos. Thus looping video helps the user have a feeling of engagement with this video when they comment or read comments.

  However, looping videos might influence people around by noisy earworms. Therefore I come up with an idea to make TikTok less bothering while maintaining the same immersive experience for users:
  Video should still loop as before but volume of the video should be lower once the video begins looping. And in each next loop, volume will be lower than previous loop. After 5 loops, video will be mute.
